✨ The Road Not Taken
✨ Still I Rise
✨ Fire and Ice

✨Beauty of poetry✨

Rays of sunshine filter through,
A gentle breeze, a peaceful hue.
The world awakens, fresh and bright
A new day dawns, full of delight.

In this serene and peaceful place,
I find my heart, my soul's escape.
Where love and joy, they forever reign,
And beauty's splendor, my heart sustains.

Smile, and the world smiles with you,
Cry, and you cry alone.
Spread love, and it will grow,
In the garden of your soul

Roses are red,
Violets are blue,
Sugar is sweet,
And so are you

You are a star,
Shining bright from afar.
Twinkling in the night,
Guiding me to what's right

Life is short,
Smile while you can.
Love is rare,
Hold it in your hand
